1. Site Name: Growth. The Site name "Growth" was selected because of how the website is basically all about growth. Helping users grow the their number of clients and sales.

2. Site Purpose: The purpose of the website is to provide a comprehensive, all-in-one tool designed to help startups grow and succeed. It aims to streamline various aspects of startup management, such as organization, collaboration, and progress-tracking, into single, user-friendly platform.

3. Scenarios: Scenario 1- As a busy startup founder, I'm struggling to keep track of multiple tasks and team members. How can your growth app help me streamline my workflow and increase productivity? Scenario 2- I'm a new member of a growing startup. How can i quickly get up to speed on projects and collaborate effectively with my team using your growth app?
